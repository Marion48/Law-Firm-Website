Comprehensive Compensation & Liability Representation

When accidents, injuries, or losses occur due to another party's negligence or wrongdoing, securing fair compensation becomes essential. At Byron N. & Co. Advocates, we combine deep legal expertise with compassionate client care to navigate complex liability matters.

Our approach focuses on thorough investigation, strategic negotiation, and when necessary, assertive litigation to protect your rights and secure the compensation you deserve for medical expenses, lost income, pain and suffering, and other damages.

Personal Injury Claims

When you suffer physical or psychological harm due to someone else's negligence, you have the right to seek compensation. Our personal injury practice covers a wide range of incidents where duty of care has been breached.

Comprehensive Case Evaluation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your case, gathering evidence, medical records, and witness statements to establish liability and quantify damages.

Strategic Claim Development

Our team develops a compelling claim strategy, working with medical experts, accident reconstruction specialists, and economic analysts to build a strong case.

Negotiation & Settlement

We engage in strategic negotiations with insurance companies and opposing counsel, aiming for fair settlement while preparing for trial if necessary.

Medical Negligence & Malpractice

When healthcare providers fail to meet accepted standards of care, causing injury or worsening medical conditions, we pursue accountability through medical negligence claims.

Surgical Errors

Wrong-site surgery, unnecessary procedures, retained surgical instruments, and anesthesia errors.

Diagnostic Failures

Failure to diagnose, delayed diagnosis, misdiagnosis of serious conditions including cancer and heart disease.

Treatment Errors

Medication errors, improper treatment, failure to obtain informed consent, and substandard postoperative care.
