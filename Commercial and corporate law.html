Commercial & Corporate Services

Running a business comes with both opportunities and challenges. At Byron N & Co Advocates, we help your business navigate the law and market with clear advice, strong legal protection, and practical solutions that support your growth. Whether you're starting a new venture, signing important agreements, or handling a sensitive business issue, we make sure you understand your options and make decisions confidently.

01

Business Setup & Structuring

Starting a company or partnership shouldn't feel confusing.

We help you

  • Choose the right business structure
  • Register your business and draft essential documents
  • Set up shareholder or partnership arrangements

Outcome: A solid legal foundation that protects your interests from day one.

02

Contracts & Agreements

Every successful business relies on clear agreements.

We prepare, review, and negotiate

  • Supplier and service contracts
  • Employment and consultancy agreements
  • Shareholder and partnership agreements
  • Confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements

Outcome: Strong, easy-to-understand contracts that reduce risk and protect your business.

03

Compliance & Regulatory Support

We guide you through the rules and obligations that affect your industry.

We assist with

  • Licensing and regulatory filings
  • Corporate governance
  • Legal audits and compliance checks

Outcome: Peace of mind knowing your business meets all legal requirements.

04

Mergers, Acquisitions & Business Transfers

Whether you're buying, selling, or restructuring a business, you need clear advice and careful planning.

We guide you through

  • Due diligence
  • Negotiation and documentation
  • Post-transaction support

Outcome: Smooth transitions that protect value and reduce unexpected risks.

05

Managing Business Risks & Disputes

When issues arise, we help you address them quickly and strategically.

We support you with

  • Contract disputes
  • Shareholder or director disagreements
  • Debt recovery
  • Mediation, arbitration, or litigation if needed

Outcome: Practical solutions that keep your business stable and moving forward.

06

Ongoing Legal Support for Businesses

For growing companies, legal needs don't stop after registration.

We offer

  • On-call legal advice
  • Review of ongoing contracts
  • Support with day-to-day business decisions

Outcome: A long-term partner who helps your business stay protected as it grows.
