Litigation & Dispute Resolution Services

When a dispute threatens your peace of mind, business, or reputation, you need clear guidance and a steady hand. At Byron N & Co. Advocates, we help you understand your options, protect your interests, and reach a solution that works, whether that means negotiating a quick settlement or taking the matter to court.

01

Business & Commercial Disputes

When business disagreements arise, we step in to protect your investment and keep things moving.

We help with

  • Broken agreements or unpaid invoices
  • Conflicts between partners or shareholders
  • Supplier or service-provider disputes
  • Urgent court orders to stop harmful actions
  • Recovering money or enforcing contracts

Our goal: Get your business back on track with minimal disruption.

02

Civil Disputes

We help individuals and organisations deal with everyday conflicts that affect finances, property, or reputation.

This includes

  • Property and land disagreements
  • Claims for financial loss or negligence
  • Boundary issues or trespass
  • Landlord–tenant conflicts
  • Defamation and reputation protection

Our focus: Fair solutions and strong protection of your rights.

03

Criminal Defence

If you're facing criminal allegations, you need support you can trust.

We provide

  • Guidance during investigations
  • Applications for bail or bond
  • Defence strategy and representation in court
  • Help navigating plea arrangements

Our aim: Safeguard your freedom, dignity, and constitutional rights.

04

Constitutional & Public Law

When your rights have been violated or a public body has acted unfairly, we help you challenge the decision.

We handle

  • Claims for violation of fundamental rights
  • Challenges to unlawful government decisions
  • Public interest and community matters

What you get: Clear advice, strong arguments, and a structured plan.

Employment Disputes

Workplace conflicts can be stressful and costly.

We assist with

  • Unfair termination
  • Unpaid benefits or disputes over contracts
  • Workplace harassment or discrimination claims
  • Representation before labour courts

Our purpose: Ensure you are treated fairly and your rights are respected.

  • Representation before tax tribunals

  • Representation in arbitration
